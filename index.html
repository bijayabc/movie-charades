How To Play

Charades is a game where players split into teams and take turns acting out words or phrases without speaking, using only gestures and movements to convey the clue to their teammates. The acting team tries to guess the correct answer within a time limit (60 seconds above), while the actor can indicate correct guesses with nods or shakes. Each correct guess earns the team a point, and teams alternate roles after each round. The game continues until a team reaches a predetermined score, who is then crowned winner.
